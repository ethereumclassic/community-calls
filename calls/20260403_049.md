### Bitmain AMA Debrief

Only the first half of the AMA was recorded due to a technical issue. The full recording will be uploaded soon. The [first section is available here](https://x.com/ETCGrantsDao/status/2036001252123811929).

During the AMA, [nolympia.dev](https://nolympia.dev) was introduced — a petition opposing the Olympia treasury proposal (ECIPs 1111–1119), which would redirect transaction fees to an on-chain funding contract. The petition argues this contradicts ETC's foundational principles by introducing a protocol-enforced tax on miners and a complex governance system. It currently has 20 signatories.

### EIP-7702 Readiness

Type 0x04 transactions use EIP-1559 fee fields, not legacy gasPrice. Since ETC doesn't support type 0x02, how does the current core-geth build handle tx acceptance on that path? Has this been tested? Worth confirming with Diego before declaring 7702 feature-complete, and before Mordor deployment.

### ECIP-1121 Status

[ECIP-1121](https://github.com/ethereumclassic/ECIPs/blob/master/_specs/ecip-1121.md) is still in Draft and will be amended slightly. For a June 1 mainnet target, does it need to move to Last Call soon? Both sides appear to agree on 1121 — the divergence is between 1120 and 1110. Does ECIP-1121 need an update to reflect the current consensus?

### GravityLabs Proposals Review

As [discussed in call 48](/calls/48), dedicated session to review the Elysium ECIPs ([PR #568](https://github.com/ethereumclassic/ECIPs/pull/568), [#569](https://github.com/ethereumclassic/ECIPs/pull/569), [#570](https://github.com/ethereumclassic/ECIPs/pull/570)) and the quantum resistance precompile proposal ([PR #557](https://github.com/ethereumclassic/ECIPs/pull/557)). Has the community had a chance to review these?
